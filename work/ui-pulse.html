Background
A web financial dashboard for business users to perform cross-border transactions with their customers
MoneyMatch Sdn.Bhd. is a startup which facilitate cross-border transactions for both normal and business users. It provides a cheaper alternative than banks for making transactions.
In 2021, they launched a platform allowing business customers to perform bulk transactions for their target recipients. These transactions are often in huge amount. This platform is called Pulse.
* Image is extracted from the support site.
Design Overhaul
A modern look to represent a successful expansion of MoneyMatch
The platform has been developed with a basic interface to accommodate necessary functions. The next phase was a UI design overhaul. However, I was only available for a few of the features implemented. The designs that have been developed and can be shared are shown below.
